Who is he?

Silas Morgan, commonly known as "Shepherd" is a player character for D&D live table campaign "Curse of Srathdanya" from Legends of Avantris. This character is played by Andy, one of the oldest memebers of said D&D group.


Shepherd is a tiefling from a isolated village called Rattlesnake, Yona("wild west"). He grew up in said village, where after his parents were burned alive for blasphemy (fake claims), was raised by Dr. Thomas Cogburn, a talented artificer who teached Shep how to use two arcane pistols called Judgement and Redemption. With time he became skilled with said guns, and started to defend the village to not only protect the village and Dr. Cogburn, but to prove tha tiefligs are not bad people. He became known as the

"Devil of the Plains"
.

After some time he got emplyed by Prof. Clayton Azran to protect the Azran expedition for danger while on the search for a lost city called Barovia, even tho he didnt really agree, he wanted the money and decided to join the rest of the expedition, with Victoria Isaacs, Kana Soyokaze, and Sarnax of the Edelwood, inmediately clashing with the last one.
